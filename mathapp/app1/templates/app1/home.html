On this page, you will find an index of mathematical content (the table on the left) that is intended for high school students in the Ontario school board. This site is a work in progress, so check back at a later time if you find that one of the sections you are looking for information on is not yet available. A few things you should note if you plan on utilizing this site:

  • The notes are purposely condensed (a TLDR version, so to speak) and are not intended to be a full lesson of the concepts covered in the quizzes.
  • The quizzes are multiple choice only at the moment. However, this does not mean that every question is simply knowledge based. You are strongly recommended to write out your process work on a seperate sheet of paper before you select an answer to check if you are correct. Most tests are not multiple choice and require you to show your work.
  • The questions also contain hints, but I strongly suggest you attempt the question first before looking at any of the hints.








You can also create an account so you can make posts in the forum where you can discuss the notes and quizzes with other users. Perhaps there is a question you cannot solve and need assistance. Or maybe you are confident in your solution, and the solution is not showing up in the multiple choice. In that case, you are certainly encouraged to post your finding on the forum and maybe you have found a mistake. General feedback for the website is also appreciated.
