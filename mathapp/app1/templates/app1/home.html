Welcome to the site. On this page, you will find an index of mathematical content (the table on the left) that is intended for high school students in the Ontario school board. This site is a work in progress, so check back at a later time if you find that one of the sections you are looking for information on is not yet available. A few things you should note if you plan on utilising this site:

  • The notes are purposely condensed (a TLDR version, so to speak) and are not intended to be a full lesson of the concepts covered in the quizzes. If you want access to that, you can visit my other website here.
  • The quizzes are multiple choice only at the moment. However, this does not mean that every question is simply knowledge based. Certain problems may require you to work out detailed solutions to arrive at the correct answer.
  • The questions also contain hints, but I strongly suggest you attempt the question first before looking at any of the hints.
