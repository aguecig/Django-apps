Identities of logarithms

  • Product rule
    \log{_{b}(xy)} = \log{_{b}x} + \log{_{b}y}
  • Quotient rule
    \log{_{b}\left( \frac{x}{y} \right)} = \log{_{b}x} - \log{_{b}y}
  • Power rule
    \log{_{b}x}^{n} = n\log{_{b}x}
  • Log 1 of any base
    \log{_{b}1} = 0
  • Log b of base b
    \log{_{b}b} = 1
  • Log of any base
    \log{_{b}a} = \frac{\log{a}}{\log{b}}
