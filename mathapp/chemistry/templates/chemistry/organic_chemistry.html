Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes, Alcohols and Cyclo Alkanes

Hydrogen = Blue

Carbon = Black

When you are ready, enter your answers in the text fields below and hit submit to see how you did. Keep in mind that the answers will require perfect grammatical syntax in order to be correct. Here are some examples:

  • 2,3-dimethylhexane would be considered correct, but 2,3 dimethylhexane would not, since it is missing the -
  • pentan-1-ene would be correct, but Pentan-1-ene would not since it contains a captial letter
  • 3-ethylnonane is correct, but 3-ethyl nonane is not because it contains a space
